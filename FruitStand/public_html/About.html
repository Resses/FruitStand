ABOUT

Hi! My name is Renee, and I created FRUIT STAND as a fun way to help kids learn about when different fruits and vegetables are in season, as well as how to make good business decisions.

STEM is an acronym for Science, Technology, Engineering and Math education. It is very important for kids to be educated in these fields, especially through fun ways like games.

To learn more, visit:
  • STEM at cslnet.org
  • Seasonal Fruits at cuesa.org
